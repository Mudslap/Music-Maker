About Music Maker

An easy application for anyone inspired to create music!

Easy to Use!

Thanks to our hard work and incredible use of Javascript, and other resources, Music Maker is one of the easiest music softwares to date! All you have to do is click a few buttons and watch a short tutorial to completely master the application.

Completely Free!

Music Maker is completely free, meaning that whoever wants to, can access the application at any time and create any music they want!

No time at all!

Thanks to our simple tutorial and clicking mechanism, this allows even beginers to start making and editing music is no time at all!
